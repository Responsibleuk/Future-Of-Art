    Where art and science meet

WHAT WE DO

SCIENCE

At their core, art and science are both about observation and interpretation. Whether it is by using scientific data or technologies such as AI and VR to create art or by using art to understand and communicate science, both work hand in hand.

TECHNOLOGY

We stand on the brink of a technological revolution that will fundamentally alter the way we live, work, and relate to one another. In its scale, scope, and complexity, the transformation will be unlike anything humankind has experienced before.

ENGINEERING

Cornwall's mining heritage is a rich and fascinating subject, and the intersection of art and engineering within it provides a unique perspective on the history of this region.

ART

We are excited to see the innovative projects that will result from the marriage of art and science, technology, engineering and mathematics. Join us in discovering the art of innovation and transforming the way we approach problem-solving.

MATHS

Line, shape, form, pattern, symmetry, scale and proportion are the building blocks of both art and maths. Geometry offers the most obvious connection between the two disciplines.
